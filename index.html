Naya Gaun Church

Nayagaun Church exists to lead people to real transformation as they love god and change their world. The apostolic creed is the basic for our bibical doctrines.

This Church is located in pokhara 15 NayaGaun. It was established in 1987. It has Approximately 1000 members. It is an independent church and affilliated with nepal churches Fellowship of nepal and pokhara chirstain community. The church is headed by a leadership team of a pastor,10 elders and 10 deacons. It has 11 daughter churches in kaski, Tanahunm, Parbat districts. There are 7 full times and 1 part time staff members including the pastor.
